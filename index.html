I design products across the physical and digital world, from injection moulded hardware to the interfaces that power them, drawing on nearly a decade of academic and professional work for clients and companies.

Core skills span user research, journey mapping, usability testing, 3D modeling in Rhino, rendering, and translating pain points into intuitive interfaces, backed by Figma, front end coding, and rapid prototyping, with a proven track record leading projects from concept to launch across cross functional and cross border teams.
