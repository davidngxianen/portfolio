LM360: A Liquid Cooler Built Around Its Display

LM360 is a liquid cooler pump block designed for DeepCool, built around a compact display, using light and screen content, rather than surface detailing alone, to give the pump a sense of presence inside a build.

YEAR 2024/2025
ROLE Lead Industrial Designer
TOOLS KeyShot, Rhino, Photoshop, Illustrator
TIMELINE 5 months
LAST REVISED 2025
01 / OVERVIEW

A screen, not just a shroud

The LM series brings a fresh update to liquid cooling with a compact 2.4 inch IPS display that now supports MP4 video playback for a more dynamic screen experience. Work on LM360 ran across five months, led from the earliest design direction through to design for manufacturing.

Built for clarity

A 320 x 240 resolution ensures crisp visuals, while enhanced color accuracy and screen stability deliver consistent clarity throughout. The soft perimeter lighting adds structured contrast to the minimal design.

02 / THE POWER SOURCE EFFECT

Making the pump feel like the source

Beyond the screen itself, the lighting was treated as part of the product's core identity rather than decoration added on top.

At the heart of the design is a softly illuminated core that appears to radiate energy outward through the layered structure. This lighting effect creates the impression that the pump is the power source of the cooling system, giving the product a sense of motion and vitality while maintaining a clean, sophisticated aesthetic.

03 / CONCEPT GENERATION

From an ambitious first pass to something buildable

The following concept represents the initial design direction for the LM360 series.

The original proposal explored a more premium and futuristic aesthetic, featuring layered geometries and intricate surface details that created a bold visual identity and a strong sense of depth. However, to better align with the target market, the concept was simplified to reduce production costs, improve manufacturability, and streamline assembly while preserving the core design language. This iterative process demonstrates the balance between ambitious design vision and the practical realities of mass production.

04 / OUTCOME & REFLECTION

What this project made possible

LM360 pushed the pump block from a purely functional shroud into the visual centerpiece of a build, using screen content and layered light rather than surface detailing alone to do that work. Leading the project from the first concept through to design for manufacturing meant weighing an ambitious early direction against what could actually be produced, and finding a simplified form that kept the design language intact.
