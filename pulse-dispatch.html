03 / EMPATHY MAP

Calm under a flood of information

Placeholder text — summarize the dispatcher's mental state and what the interface needs to protect against.

The Dispatcher

Overloaded, not overwhelmed

  • Handling multiple live calls at once
  • Needs signal over noise — not more data, the right data
  • Must triage severity in seconds
  • Relies on muscle memory; UI changes must not break it
The System

What the console must protect

  • Never let a critical call get visually buried
  • Surface AI transcript highlights, not raw text walls
  • Make responder coordination one glance, not a search
  • Fail gracefully — never blank-screen during a live call
